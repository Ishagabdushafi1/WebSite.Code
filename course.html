Our Courses

We offer a wide range of courses in Computing and Design. Our courses are designed to provide students with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed in their chosen career paths. Each course is taught by experienced faculty members who are experts in their fields. We also provide practical learning opportunities through labs, workshops, and industry projects to ensure our students are well- prepared for the job market. Below is an overview of the undergraduate and postgraduate courses we offer.

    Undergraduate Courses

    BSc Computing Courses

    The College offers a range of courses to suit applicants with varying backgrounds and educational abilities. At undergraduate level, there are single BSc Honours Degree courses in Computing, Computer Networks, Software Engineering and Cyber Security with Forensics amongst others.

    BA Art and Design

    The College's art and design courses don't just train you, they promote alternative ways of thinking, making and communicating; they provide you with space, tools and inspiration to develop your creative practice and a clear career path. You'll get expert teaching from active practitioners and researchers who will encourage you to adopt innovative and resourceful approaches that both perceive and create opportunities for better lives

    Postgraduate Courses

    MSc Advanced Computing

    Focuses on advanced topics such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science. The courses are British Computer Society accredited and are highly relevant to modern industry. They are designed to prepare students for professional occupations in Computing and related fields. Many graduates continue their studies to pursue a higher degree such as an MSc. or PhD.

    MA Atr and Design

    Our MA Art and Design course is designed to help you develop a professional art and design practice at an advanced level. You’ll develop your creative practice whilst interacting with your peers in well-equipped studios, making and digital workshops. At the same time, you'll learn professional skills by working on applied briefs facilitated through our links with commercial clients, cultural institutions, businesses and organisations.
